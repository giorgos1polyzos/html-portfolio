The Best Movies According to Giorgos

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Inception

Inception is a mind-bending thriller that explores the depths of the subconscious. It's visually stunning and conceptually ambitious, keeping you on the edge of your seat as it delves into layers of dreams within dreams. The intricate plot and brilliant performances make it a captivating cinematic experience.

The Dark Knight

The Dark Knight is not just a superhero movie; it's a gritty, intense crime drama that transcends the genre. Heath Ledger's portrayal of the Joker is iconic, adding a layer of complexity to the film's exploration of chaos versus order. It's a dark and gripping masterpiece that challenges the conventional notions of heroism.

Midnight Express

Midnight Express is a gripping and harrowing tale of survival in a foreign land. Based on a true story, it immerses you in the protagonist's desperate struggle for freedom and justice. The tension is palpable throughout, making it a compelling and unforgettable cinematic journey.
